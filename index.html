What I Do

Custom native planting plans

I help Burlington-area homeowners create yards full of native plants and pollinators. Beautiful spaces that are easy to care for and alive with bees, butterflies, and birds.

As an Environmental Science student with a passion for Vermont's local ecology, I design custom planting plans that tell you exactly what to plant and where, using native and pollinator-friendly species suited to our climate. You get expert, ecological design without the cost of a full-service landscaping crew. Perfect for anyone who wants a thriving, low-maintenance yard they'll love for years.

Why Native

Beautiful for you, vital for Vermont

Native plants are adapted to our climate, so once established they need little watering or fuss, saving you time and money. They also support the birds, bees, and butterflies that local ecosystems depend on. A native planting is a garden that gives back: lower maintenance for you, and a real haven for Vermont's pollinators.
